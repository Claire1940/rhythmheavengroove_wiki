### Scoring and Medal Requirements

Rhythm Heaven Groove rates your performance at the end of every minigame. Understanding these ranks is crucial for unlocking rewards in the full game.

| Rank Awarded | Score Range | Medal Earned | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Perfect** | **100% Hits** | **Perfect Ribbon** | Earned by completing a stage without a single mistake during a Perfect Campaign. |
| **Superb** | **85% - 99%** | **Gold Medal** | Excellent rhythm kept, very few minor misses. Unlocks Beatspell chapters. |
| **OK** | **60% - 84%** | **Silver Medal** | Passed the stage, but with noticeable timing errors. |
| **Try Again** | **0% - 59%** | **None** | Failed to keep the beat. Requires more practice on timing cues. |
