<Section icon={Target} title="Console Setup & TV Calibration Guide">

Because Rhythm Heaven Groove relies entirely on frame-perfect inputs rather than visual reaction speed, input lag can completely ruin your experience. Standard modern televisions process images in a way that introduces minor audio and visual delays. To combat this, the developers have included an in-game calibration setting to sync your button presses precisely with the music.

Follow these steps to optimize your hardware setup for the best rhythmic performance:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable TV Game Mode">
    Navigate to your television's picture settings menu and activate **Game Mode** to minimize internal video processing latency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access Calibration Menu">
    Launch the game on your Nintendo Switch and head to **Settings > Sound & Calibration**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Match the Audio Beats">
    Close your eyes and tap the button exactly when you hear the snare drum cue, ignoring any visual prompts on the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Save Your Millisecond Offset">
    Verify your adjusted latency score (shown in milliseconds) and save the configuration before entering any minigames.
  </Step>
</Steps>
