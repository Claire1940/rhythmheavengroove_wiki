Rhythm games require absolute precision. Because modern flat-screen TVs introduce varying levels of display lag, configuring your game correctly is crucial to earning those elusive "Superb" ratings [1].

### Configuring Local Multiplayer (1-4 Players)
The multiplayer suite supports up to four players on a single Nintendo Switch console [1]. 
- **Controller Requirements**: Each player requires one compatible controller (such as a single Joy-Con or a Pro Controller) [1]. 
- **Supported Modes**: TV Mode and Tabletop Mode are highly recommended [1]. Handheld multiplayer is supported but can feel cramped [1].
- **No Online Play**: Note that local wireless and online multiplayer are not supported; all play is local on a single screen [1].
