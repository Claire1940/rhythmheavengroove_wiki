<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beatspell Mode: The Rhythm-RPG Adventure">

A major addition to the franchise is **Beatspell**, a story-driven single-player rhythm-RPG mode. In this mode, players step into the shoes of a young magician embarking on a fantasy quest. Instead of standard musical stages, you engage in turn-based monster battles where your attacks, defenses, and spells are executed by chaining rhythmic button presses.

### Beatspell Battle Mechanics
- **Unlocking Chapters**: New story chapters are unlocked by earning **Medals** in the main solo campaign.
- **Spell Variety**: Learn different spells as you progress, ranging from offensive fireballs to critical healing spells.
- **Rhythm Chaining**: Inputting commands perfectly in sync with the combat music increases your spell damage and chips away at enemy health bars.
- **Penalty for Mistakes**: Missing a beat disrupts your spell combo, leaving you vulnerable to enemy counterattacks.
