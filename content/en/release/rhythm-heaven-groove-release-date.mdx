<Section icon={Shield} title="Rhythm Heaven Groove Beginner Guide">

Rhythm Heaven Groove is built entirely around **rhythm and timing**, not reaction speed. Visuals are often designed to distract or trick your eyes, meaning your ears are your most valuable asset. Use these foundational strategies to earn Superb ratings and unlock medals.

| Tip Category | Recommended Strategy | Game Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Audio-First Play** | **Close your eyes** during tricky segments and focus purely on the drum beats. | Bypasses visual distractions and visual delay. |
| **Visual Cues** | Treat animations as secondary feedback rather than primary reaction prompts. | Prevents early button presses caused by screen delay. |
| **Calibration** | Run the timing wizard in the options menu whenever you change displays. | Aligns your physical button presses with the game's internal clock. |
| **Remix Mastery** | Replay early stages to memorize individual game tempos before attempting Remixes. | Prepares you for sudden transitions between different minigames. |

<Callout type="success" title="The Golden Rule of Rhythm Heaven">
Always prioritize the audio cues over what is happening on screen. Many advanced stages will actively obscure the screen or shake the camera to test if you are truly feeling the beat rather than just watching the animations.
</Callout>

</Section>
