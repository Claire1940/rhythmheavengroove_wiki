<Section icon={Swords} title="Controls and Coordination Mechanics">

Unlike standard single-button rhythm games, Crab Snacks requires you to coordinate two distinct inputs simultaneously. Since you control both the left and right claws of the crab, you must press both buttons at the exact same moment to execute a successful bounce.

| Action | Control Input | Role in Gameplay |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Right Claw** | Ⓐ Button | Moves the right claw up to bounce incoming food. |
| **Left Claw** | ✚ / ◀ D-Pad Button | Moves the left claw up to bounce incoming food. |
| **Dual Sync** | Ⓐ + ◀ simultaneously | Required to catch and stash the snacks cleanly. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Bilateral Coordination Warning">
If your left claw does not move in sync with your right claw, the snack will slip through. Make sure you press both Ⓐ and Left on the D-Pad at the exact same frame.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Perfect Rank">

Achieving a Perfect rank requires completing the stage without a single "Early!", "Late!", or "Miss..." input. Follow this progression to secure your medal.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Get a Feel for the Claws">
    Before the music starts, press Ⓐ and ◀ D-Pad five times to calibrate your hands and ensure your inputs register simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Food Visuals">
    Watch the right side of the screen. Spotting whether a macaron or a mangosteen is rolling in determines your timing strategy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Count the Beats Internally">
    Tap your foot to the steady background tempo. For macarons, press on the next whole beat. For mangosteens, wait for the off-beat (the "and" of the beat).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Bounce and Stash">
    Press both buttons firmly right as the snack reaches your crab's position. If successful, you will hear a clean "Perfect!" or "Ace!" sound effect.
  </Step>
