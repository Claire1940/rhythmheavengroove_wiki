In *Rhythm Heaven Groove*, achieving a perfect rating is vastly different from simply playing flawlessly during normal practice. These campaigns are randomly hosted on cleared stages, demanding absolute precision where a single missed beat or off-tempo hit results in immediate failure.

Understanding the campaign's lifespan is critical for managing your attempts. The campaign interface features a distinct border decorated with hearts and counter lines to indicate your remaining opportunities.

| Campaign Mechanic | System Rules | Player Impact |
