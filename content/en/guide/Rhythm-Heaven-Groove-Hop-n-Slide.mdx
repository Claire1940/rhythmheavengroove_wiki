### Step-by-Step Execution Guide

Follow these steps to train your muscle memory for the perfect run:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Internalize the Basic Beat">
    Before pressing any buttons, listen to the snappy snare drum in the background song. Tap your foot to build a steady internal metronome.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Vocal Cue">
    Listen closely for the vocal pattern: "Hop, hop, bumble-rumble!" The "hop, hop" requires two single taps, while "bumble-rumble" requires a rapid double-tap to initiate the rolling animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ignore the Kaleidoscope Beach Shift">
    Halfway through the song, the background shifts from a wood-paneled room to a bright beach. Keep your eyes focused on the cat doll itself or look slightly past the screen to let your peripheral vision handle the visual changes while you focus on the audio.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate the Glitch Phase">
    When the screen begins to warp and speed up near the end, ignore the chaotic movement. Keep your tapping speed identical to the initial tempo.
  </Step>
</Steps>
