<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Scoring a Perfect">

Earning a Perfect rating on any of the **rhythm heaven groove remixes** requires absolute precision. When a "Perfect Campaign" randomly spawns on a level, you are given three attempts to clear the stage without a single mistake. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Gold Medal First">
    Before you can attempt a Perfect run, you must clear the stage with a high enough score to earn a standard Gold Medal. This unlocks the ability for that stage to host Perfect Campaigns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Analyze the Transition Points">
    Most players fail their Perfect runs during the visual transition between minigames. Memorize which game comes next so your fingers are pre-positioned for the correct button layout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Listen for the Snappy Snare Cue">
    The sound design in this entry features a highly distinct, loud snare feedback on perfect hits. If your button presses do not align with this crisp audio pop, slightly adjust your latency settings in the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Night Mode">
    Once you earn a Perfect rating, you can unlock the highly coveted **Night Mode** variant. To achieve a Rainbow Perfect, you must complete three consecutive Perfect runs in Night Mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Latency Calibration">
If you are playing on a docked Nintendo Switch, TV display lag can ruin your timing. We highly recommend playing in handheld mode or calibrating your audio offset in the settings before attempting Backside Perfect runs.
</Callout>

</Section>
