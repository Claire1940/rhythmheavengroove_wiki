<Section icon={Trophy} title="Unlocking the Full Soundtrack Library">

To fully experience the **rhythm heaven groove ost**, you must progress through the main campaign. Unlike previous entries that relied heavily on a linear story, this game uses a grid-based system where performance directly impacts your progression speed. Medals are the primary currency for the single-player library, while Rings are essential for the multiplayer and side-mode tracks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Achieve Amazing Ranks">
    Play through the standard rhythm games. Scoring an "Amazing!" rank (or "Superb" on Flipside) grants you a Medal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Rings in Co-op">
    Engage in multiplayer games like "Tennis Quest" or "Sick Beats" to earn Rings. These are used to purchase side modes in the shop.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Remix Sets">
    Unlock Remix 1 through 20 by completing the four prerequisite games in each set. Remixes combine all previous musical themes into a single track.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Visit the Soundboard">
    Once Medals and Rings are accumulated, visit the Soundboard in the Extra menu to listen to the OST without gameplay distractions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success">
Completing a Perfect Campaign (where the game challenges you to play a track without a single mistake) unlocks the "Original Version" of the song in the Soundboard, often featuring lyrics or extended intros.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Legacy and Technical Evolution">

The 2026 release of *Rhythm Heaven Groove* introduces several technical firsts for the series. It is the first title to be localized in multiple new languages, including Chinese and Dutch, and the first to feature full voice acting during cutscenes. This adds a layer of depth to the **rhythm heaven groove ost**, as characters now have distinct vocal cues that sync with the background music.

| Feature | Rhythm Heaven (DS) | Rhythm Heaven Groove (Switch) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Total Save Files | 3 | 1 (Per User Profile) |
| Voice Acting | Minimal | Full Cutscenes/Menus |
| Control Scheme | Touch/Stylus | Buttons (A, B, D-Pad) |
| Localization | 5 Languages | 10+ Languages |
| Remix Count | 10 | 20 |

The game also utilizes the "VoiceText" synthesizer for Lil' Miss Reeds, the same technology seen in *Tomodachi Life*. This allows the game to dynamically generate dialogue that stays perfectly in time with the BPM of whichever track is currently playing.
