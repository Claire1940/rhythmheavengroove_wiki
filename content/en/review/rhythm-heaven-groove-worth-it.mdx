<Section icon={Swords} title="Beatspell Mode & Multiplayer Features">

In addition to the standard rhythm trials, Rhythm Heaven Groove introduces **Beatspell Mode**, a single-player rhythm-RPG hybrid. In this mode, you take on the role of a magician traversing a monster-filled cave. Button prompts are mapped to elemental spells, allowing you to attack, heal, and defend based on your rhythmic accuracy.

### Local Multiplayer Suite
For social gatherings, the game features over **30 multiplayer games** that support up to four local players on a single system. These games are split between collaborative challenges and competitive timing battles.
