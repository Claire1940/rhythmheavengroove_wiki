Unlike traditional rhythm games that feature busy note highways, this title keeps the screen clean and expects players to rely almost entirely on their ears. <Section icon={Target} title="Beatspell Mode & Multiplayer Madness">

Beyond the standard solo campaign, **Rhythm Heaven Groove** introduces two major pillars of additional content: the unlockable **Beatspell RPG Mode** and a robust local party mode.

### Beatspell: A Rhythmic RPG Journey
Unlocked as you progress through the main solo campaign, Beatspell plays like a classic StreetPass RPG. Players journey down elemental paths, encountering various monsters that must be defeated using rhythmic spellcasting.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Mode">
    Progress through the main solo campaign and earn medals to open the Beatspell chapter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Spells">
    Acquire rhythmic magic spells, items, and passive stat buffs before heading out.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fight on the Beat">
    Cast spells, trigger elemental attacks, and heal your character by tapping buttons in perfect time with enemy rhythm cues.
  </Step>

### Local Multiplayer Chaos
The local co-op and competitive mode supports up to 4 players on a single system, making it an instant party essential.

| Multiplayer Event | Mode Type | Players | Core Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rhythm Tweezers** | Cooperative | 1–4 | Pluck hairs in perfect alternating sequence |
| **Tennis Quest** | Cooperative | 1–4 | Defeat monsters together by timing tennis swings |
| **Cake Wait** | Competitive | 2–4 | Grab the cake at the exact microsecond the timer hits 3 |
| **Lucha Libre Race** | Competitive | 2–4 | Race across giant bouncy balls as luchadores |

<Callout type="warning" title="Input Lag and TV Calibration">
Docked TV play can introduce minor input lag that ruins your timing. Use the in-game calibration settings immediately when switching to a TV, or play in Tabletop/Handheld mode for direct, lag-free input response.
</Callout>
