<Section icon={Shield} title="Rhythm Heaven Groove Switch Lite: Compatibility Overview">

Rhythm Heaven Groove is officially compatible with the entire Nintendo Switch family, including the standard Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch OLED, and Nintendo Switch Lite. Since the game relies primarily on precise button presses timed to musical beats rather than complex motion controls, Switch Lite players can experience the vast majority of the game without any additional hardware. 

However, because the Switch Lite is a dedicated handheld system with non-detachable controls, certain play styles, particularly local multiplayer, require specific hardware configurations to function. Below is a complete compatibility breakdown for standard play styles.

| Feature / Mode | Switch Lite Compatibility | Additional Hardware Required? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter Demo** | Fully Supported | No, plays perfectly in Handheld Mode |
| **80+ Solo Rhythm Games** | Fully Supported | No, uses built-in face buttons and D-pad |
| **Beatspell Mode** | Fully Supported | No, standard button inputs only |
| **Local Multiplayer (2-4 Players)** | Supported with Setup | Yes, external Joy-Cons or Pro Controllers |
| **Audio Calibration** | Fully Supported | Recommended to use wired headphones |

<Callout type="success" title="Wired Audio is Highly Recommended">
For the absolute best timing on the Nintendo Switch Lite, use wired headphones. Bluetooth audio can introduce minor latency that compromises your ability to secure "Superb" ratings in high-tempo minigames.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire External Controllers">
    Obtain compatible wireless controllers, such as a pair of Nintendo Switch Joy-Cons or a Nintendo Switch Pro Controller, for each additional player.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Sync Controllers to the Switch Lite">
    Navigate to the Controllers menu on the Nintendo Switch home screen. Select Change Grip/Order and press the sync button on your external controllers to pair them with your Switch Lite.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Set Up a Tabletop Stand">
    Because the Switch Lite lacks a built-in kickstand, prop the console up using an adjustable charging stand or a secure surface so all players can clearly view the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Launch Multiplayer Mode">
    Open Rhythm Heaven Groove, select the Multiplayer menu, and assign each synced controller to a player to begin cooperative challenges like Rhythm Tweezers.
  </Step>
</Steps>
